Frequently Asked Questions

Take a look and find the right one for you!

What is RoomieTasks?

RoomieTasks is a web application designed to help roommates, families, and households manage shared tasks and responsibilities. It provides a simple, intuitive platform for creating, assigning, and tracking household chores to ensure everyone contributes fairly to maintaining your shared living space.

How do I create a household?

After registering and logging in, navigate to the Households section in your dashboard. Click on the "Create Household" card, enter a name for your household, and click "Create". You'll automatically become the owner of the household and will receive an invite code to share with your roommates so they can join.

How do I join an existing household?

To join an existing household, you'll need to obtain the invite code from the household owner. Once you have the code, go to the Households section in your dashboard, click on the "Join Household" card, enter the invite code, and click "Join". You'll immediately be added as a member of that household.

How do I create and assign tasks?

From your household page, click on "Manage Tasks", then click the "Add Task" button. Fill in the task details including title, description, due date, and select a household member to assign it to. You can also set the priority level and make tasks recurring (daily, weekly, or monthly). Click "Create Task" to add it to your household's task list.

What happens when I mark a recurring task as completed?

When you mark a recurring task as completed, it will automatically create the next occurrence based on the recurrence pattern you selected. For daily tasks, the next occurrence will be set for the following day. Weekly tasks will recur in 7 days, and monthly tasks will recur in one month. This ensures your recurring chores continue without needing to manually recreate them.

Can I leave a household?

Yes, regular members can leave a household at any time by visiting the household details page and clicking the "Leave Household" button. However, if you're the owner of the household, you cannot leave until you either transfer ownership to another member or delete the entire household. This prevents households from being left without an owner.

How did we build this beautiful UI that fast?

Thanks to some EasyFrontend (easyfrontend.com) free UI templates! We then customized and extended these templates with Angular components to create a responsive, modern application. This approach allowed us to focus on functionality and user experience while maintaining a professional appearance.

Is my data secure?

RoomieTasks takes data security seriously. All passwords are securely hashed before being stored in our database, and connections to our server are encrypted using HTTPS. We only collect the minimum information needed to provide our service, and we never share your personal data with third parties. Your household information is only accessible to members you've explicitly invited.
